Welcome to Farmanger, partner! This is a fun little app to record notes about where and what you're planting! Great for backyard farmers and those running a CSA farm!
CSA stands for Community-Supported Agriculture - where folks are able to move away from large-scale farming operations (that often times exist thousands of miles away!) to a more local, sustainable option. Watch the video below for a little more insight.
To use this app, click on the 'Manage Crops' button to view, add, and update crops you're interested in. Click on the 'Manage Fields' button to view, add, and update your farming plots!
